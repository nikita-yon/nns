ABOUT UVB-76

UVB-76, also known as Buzzer, is a Russian military radio station
that broadcasts on a frequency of 4625 kHz.
It translates monotonous, repetitive signals
at a rate of approximately 15 tones per minute,
24 hours a day. Sometimes the buzzer signal
is interrupted and the voice is transmitted in Russian.
